About Us

Remine India is a groundbreaking startup that is dedicated to reducing the negative impact of electronic waste and lithium-ion batteries on the environment. As one of the leading companies in the field of e-waste and battery recycling , Remine India is committed to promoting sustainable practices and protecting our planet's natural resources.The team at Remine India is made up of highly skilled and passionate individuals who are experts in the field of e-waste management and recycling. We use technologies and innovative techniques to efficiently and effectively recycle electronic waste and lithium-ion batteries, thus preventing them from ending up in landfills or harming the environment.
